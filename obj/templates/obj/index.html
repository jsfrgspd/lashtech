About Us

Welcome to the About page of LashTech! We are the innovative minds behind the creation of LashTech, a cutting-edge web app-based lash recommendation, virtual try-on, and seamless booking platform. We are a group of three talented individuals from BSIT 4-5 at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila. Our team consists of Mathew Flores, Catherine Ann Pilapil, and Allen Jasfer Torres.


Together, we have harnessed the power of Artificial Intelligence and Geo Location technology to bring you LashTech, an intuitive solution tailored for lash enthusiasts. Through our collaborative efforts and unwavering dedication, we have crafted a user-friendly experience that seamlessly integrates lash recommendations, virtual try-ons, and effortless bookings, all in one platform. Explore the world of lashes with LashTech, where innovation meets beauty.
